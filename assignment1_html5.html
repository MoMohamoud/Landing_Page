Video

HTML5 has the option to autoplay videos and to preload videos to save the user some time.

I've decided not to autoplay the video but to preload it for a better user experience. With the preload enabled, a user can view the video without waiting for the video to load.

In the specs, its recommended to add the following attribute for mp4 codecs type="video/mp4; 'codecs='avc1.42E01E, mp4a.40.2'" but seems like the attribute isn't recognized by Chrome. Works great on Firefox though.

To enable the control add, controls="controls"

Controls enabled by adding controls="controls"

Due to adding a video/mp4 attribute, the video is not viewable in Chrome.

type="video/mp4; 'codecs='avc1.42E01E, mp4a.40.2'"
